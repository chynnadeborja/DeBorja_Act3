    My Favorites

    Adobo

    I love adobo because it feels like home. There's just something about the way my mom makes it that makes it extra special—she adds potato and pineapple, which might sound unusual to some, but for me, it’s perfect. Every time I take a bite, it’s like I’m right back at the family table, surrounded by the warmth and comfort of home. It's more than just food; it’s all the love and memories that come with it.

    Did you like her in the morning? - NIKI

    I love Did You Like Her in the Morning? because it taps into that raw vulnerability, that bittersweet complexity of relationships. It brings out the feeling of being haunted by questions and self-doubt, wondering what someone else might have felt or thought. There's something painfully beautiful about it—how the lyrics capture that tension of wanting to know but being scared of the answer. It feels like the song is speaking directly to me, giving voice to those quiet insecurities and almost reading straight from the pages of my own journal.

    Game of Thrones

    I love Game of Thrones because it’s this incredible mix of complex characters, unpredictable storylines, and high-stakes drama that keeps me hooked. There’s something about the way the show dives into power, loyalty, and betrayal—it’s messy and brutal, but also so human. I get completely drawn into the world of Westeros, where anyone could win or lose it all in a heartbeat. The alliances, the battles, the shock of unexpected deaths—it all keeps me on the edge of my seat. Plus, it doesn’t hurt that the visuals and storytelling are just epic.
